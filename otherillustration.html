Other Illustrations

在繪製系列插畫和專案、平面設計之外，透過從日常生活中擷取畫面與經驗， 一方面尋找不同風格，也同時刺激新的靈感，進而可能發展成系列插畫的內容。 Besides making the series illustration and commissioned graphic design works, I spend lots of time drawing some little illustration which are usually the scene in my life, or just trying for new style. Through these daily practices and search, the inspiration may also become the topic of series works.
