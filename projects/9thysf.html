9th 青年影響力論壇
Youth Speak Forum in Taiwan

2016年，以「最後世代大哉問」為主題的論壇主視覺海報， 以及活動手冊編排、廣告橫幅設計。

主視覺、海報設計、手冊等
客戶：第九屆青年影響力論壇
Key visual design, poster etc.
client: 9th YSF in Taiwan
2016, June-August

第九屆台灣青年影響力論壇，主題「最後世代大哉問」，聚焦於創新科技、智慧城市 與綠色金融，討論工業4.0帶來的社會變革。主視覺以藍色與橘黃對比，呈現科技城市 和社會相輔相成，又富於競逐關係的活力，並以線條、動線等平面視覺元素呈現。

9th Youth Speak Forum in Taiwan, the topic focused on the innovative technologies, smart cities, and green finance. The main theme "The big issue from the last Generation" discussed the evolution of the society due to the industrial 4.0. For impling the feeling of the dynamics from the tense between the society and technological city, I use the contrast color and the line, moving direction to emphasize that might.
