漁蠢仨 f-OOL-ish

2018年參與教育部「尋找感動地圖」計畫， 以「漁村文化」為主題，創作團隊識別與圖標設計。

視覺識別、圖標設計
教育部「尋找感動地圖」計畫
VI, logo design
for the project (感動地圖) held by Ministry of Education, ROC
2018. July

2018年，以「漁蠢仨」團隊名稱，與友人三人參與教育部青年壯遊台灣－尋找感動地圖 企劃，進行台灣漁村人文環境探查。企劃名稱「抓漁」，試圖尋找台灣人的 「海島性」，諧音抓「魚」，亦為尋找與海親近的依海之人、尋找漁村文化。

團隊名稱也隱喻「漁村的三人」，但因我們以外人的身分進入漁村環境，便以諧音「愚蠢」 自嘲對漁村文化的駑鈍。

In 2018, my friends and I participated in the project which was held by the Ministry of Education for a little survey of the cultural environment in the fishing village in Taiwan. The project was called "catching fish(ing)", trying to find out the Marine character of Taiwanese. The project's name and the "catch fish" are homophonic in Chinese, means to looking for whom live by the sea, and the culture of fishing villages.

The team's name and the meaning of "three people in the fishing village" are also homophonic, implying our ignorance and dullness to the culture of fishing villages as an outsider.

「漁蠢仨」的識別設計，圍繞在「海洋／魚」、「愚蠢」、「人」三個概念。字型設計方面， 以手繪、拼貼創造漁村質樸的親切感，並將「魚」的元素融入其中。包含將「漁」字的 「魚」字旁還原為其象形的樣子加以變化，以及將「蠢」字旁下方的「虫」、「仨」的筆劃 改為魚的圖案。

圖案設計則結合「三個人」和海浪符號，以「人」字的象形出發，將三個人相連，形成狀似海浪， 又能看出「人」字的圖樣。

The logo design of "f-OOL-ish" derives from "ocean/fish", "ignorance", and "human" these three ideas. In terms of the design of written charcters, I use handwritting(drawing) and collages for the kindness, simple and plain of fishing villages. In this design, I also hide "fish" in the symbol, including the pictograph of "fish" in ancient Chinese letters.

The iconic design combines the symbol of "waves" and the Chinese characters "human(man)". Deriving from the pictograph of "human", the design connects three people to create the image looks like the waves and "three people" as a pun.
