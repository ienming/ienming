科技大觀園為科技部向民眾傳播科普知識 而架設的網站，2018年因科技大觀園網站改版， 重新設計logo。識別設計為英文縮寫 "STV" 的筆劃圖樣設計， 分別在三個英文字母中，隱含「磁鐵」、「槌子」和「化學試管」 三種符號，對應於象徵物理基本知識，有科學知識意涵的磁鐵；槌子 衍伸為科技、技術；化學試管則有實驗室、創新概念的含意。

Sci-Tech Vista is the website built up by Ministry of Science and Technology. The website aims to communicate scientific knowledge to the public. For the rebuild of website, this project is the new logo design for Sci-Tech Vista.

The logo design applied by the "magnet", "hammer", and "test tube" on the abbreviation of "Sci-Tech Vista". These three elements are respectively symbol of basic physics knowledge, technological and scientific ability(skill), and the meaning of innovation.

此版本根據中文字型所做。視覺呈現以幾何、粗筆畫為主，賦予如同堆積木般， 拼湊、堆疊的趣味性。顏色選用藍與黃，隱喻科技大觀園冷靜、實證的 科學精神，與黃色溫暖親近人的感受。並特別凸顯「觀」字旁，由「見」延伸 而來的眼睛和人，呈現大眾易於親近、觀看的科學知識。

This version based on the Chinese name of "Sci-Tech Vista". The thick and geometric-like strokes applies the interesting feel onto it's visual display. For impling the professional, empirical idea of "Sci-Tech Vista",and the contrast of warm and kind as a media who faces to people,I use blue and yellow to emphasize this relation.
