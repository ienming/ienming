爵士音樂社 Jass Music Club

2016年底政大爵士音樂社以「爵交」為題舉行期末公演，除了活動海報，另包含社團傳單與名片設計。

海報、名片設計
客戶：政大爵士音樂社
Poster, Member Card design
Jazz Music Club, NCCU
2016. December

替政大爵士樂社2016年年底，「爵交」期末公演所做的海報設計，透過光影與對角線、實虛對照譬喻「以爵士交友」，也以聚光燈象徵在舞台上演出的焦點重心；並透過顏色、光影營造彷彿在夜晚的小巷弄、咖啡廳中的地下爵士音樂派對。

The poster design for Jass Music Club in NCCU in december, 2016. The topic of the present means to being in relationship through Jazz music. As the whole idea comes from "being friends", I use the spotlight on the man and make his shadow as a contrast, also a "friend", as a simile. The whole color scheme try to arise the feeling of the music party at night, in the little alley or shops, like the jazz music concert underground.

名片與社團傳單主要以爵士音樂的樂器（吉他、弦）和流暢的律動為靈感。

The leaflets and member card design concepts derives mainly from the instruments, guitar and the string from guitar or cello and so on. The fluid rhythm and swing are also the inspiration.
