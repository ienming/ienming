基地影像設計 Secret Base Video Studio

VI design, graphic, logo design

2018, May

基地影像設計工作室，致力於透過影像傳達理念，將焦點放在原住民、台灣文化議題。 期望透過影像、設計與藝術等多種形式涉入，將議題轉化為清晰易懂的視覺作品。

Secret Base Video Studio focused on the aboriginal and social issues in Taiwan. Through video clips, design and visual arts, we try to convey those complex concepts and discourses to audiences for arousing public conversation.

工作室「基地」源自創始人位於台北的房子，從狹窄、陡峭的樓梯而上，一個充滿想像力、未來可能的空間出現在眼前。 透過樓梯與膠捲，象徵設計者在「基地」這個空間中的感受：陡峭、顛簸危險，但也充滿用視覺影像創造激情與衝動的可能。

Secret Base Video Studio focused on the aboriginal and social issues in Taiwan. Through video clips, design and visual arts, we try to convey those complex concepts and discourses to audiences for arousing public conversation.
