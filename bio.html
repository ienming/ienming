1996年出生在台灣新竹市，喜歡繪畫、文字。大學期間，從系上刊物製作開始接觸電腦繪圖、排版設計，後自2015年開始與朋友接案合作。2016年，在數位內容學程的美學概論課堂上再度喚起對繪畫的熱情，也進一步加強平面設計、美感欣賞的技術培養，並轉向以透明水彩創作。目前住在台北市文山區，過著午覺、游泳、研究插畫的接案生活。

Hi! I am Ien Ming. I grew up in HsinChu City, Taiwan, where I was born. I like to draw since childhood, however I stopped for a while because of the pressure of study. During my undergraduate study, I met some opportunities to get involved in digital painting and layout design at the first year, and later started to take some cases with my friends afterwards.

In 2016, the course "Aesthetics: a survey" lectured by Ms. Pei Sung aroused my passion and inspiration in painting. It also enhanced my ability in graphic design and aesthetics literacy.

From 2017, I turned to watercolour painting. Now I live a balanced freelancing life with some napping, swimming, and continuous drawing.
