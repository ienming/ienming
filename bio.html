1996年出生在台灣新竹市，小時候喜歡繪畫、文字。大學期間，從系上刊物製作開始接觸電腦繪圖、排版設計，後自2015年開始與朋友接案合作。2016年，在數位內容學程的美學概論課堂上再度喚起對繪畫的熱情，也進一步加強平面設計、美感欣賞的技術培養，並轉向以透明水彩創作。目前住在台北市文山區，過著午覺、游泳、研究插畫的接案生活。

Hi! I am Ien Ming, born and raised in HsinChu City, Taiwan. I like to draw while I was a child, however stop because of the loading of studying. During the time in the university, I have met digital painting and layout design at the first year, and started to make commision with my friends since then.

In 2016, the course "Aesthetics: a survey" lectured by Mrs. Pei Sung aroused my passion and inspiration in painting, also enhanced my ability in graphic design and the aesthetics literacy.

From 2017, I started to paint in watercolour. Now I live in WenShan district, Taipei, with a nap, swimming pool, and the life of researching drawing.
